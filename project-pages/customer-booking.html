Overview

24/7 automated booking system handling unlimited appointments across multiple channels without human intervention.

Validates customer details automatically, manages calendars for multiple operators, processes bookings, reschedules, and cancellations. Works on WhatsApp, Slack, and web widgets. Ensures timezone accuracy and scales infinitely without additional staff.

Perfect for service businesses like salons, barbershops, dental clinics, and hotels. Eliminates front-desk bottlenecks and reduces operational costs while improving customer experience.
