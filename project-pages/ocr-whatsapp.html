Overview

Complete document intake automation that transforms how legal firms collect and process client documents via WhatsApp.

How it Works

1. WhatsApp Upload

Clients upload IDs, proof of address, and statements directly through WhatsApp.

2. OCR Processing

n8n orchestrates Mistral OCR to extract document data with high accuracy.

3. Validation

System validates document quality automatically. Blurry or incomplete documents trigger re-upload prompts.

4. CRM Population

Extracts key fields (name, DOB, address, document type, amounts) and populates GoHighLevel CRM instantly.

The Challenge

Manual document reading was taking hours and prone to errors. Legal firms needed a way to process client documents faster while maintaining accuracy.

Key Outcomes

  • Speed

    Minutes To structured data from documents

  • Accuracy

    Lower Errors In intake process

  • Throughput

    Faster Cases Case throughput increased
