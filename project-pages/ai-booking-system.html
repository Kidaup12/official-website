Overview

Conversational booking system that understands natural language and manages appointments intelligently.

Connects to Cal.com for real-time availability checking, prevents invalid booking transitions with stateful memory, and integrates seamlessly with CRMs, SMS, and voice channels. Handles bookings, cancellations, and reschedules through simple conversation, eliminating booking errors and providing 24/7 scheduling automation.

Key Capabilities

Natural Language

Understands intent (book, reschedule, cancel) from conversational text.

Stateful Memory

Prevents invalid transitions and remembers context during the conversation.

Real-time Cal.com

Checks live availability and syncs bookings instantly.

Multi-channel

Works across SMS, WhatsApp, Web Chat, and Voice channels.

  • Efficiency

    Automated 24/7 Customer bookings
