Overview

AI voice agent that handles customer calls, price inquiries, and appointment bookings automatically for service businesses.

Key Features

Call Handling

Captures caller context and confirms service details in real-time.

Price Estimates

Provides accurate price estimates and ranges based on service type.

Real-time Booking

Books appointment slots in real-time with calendar integration.

CRM Integration

Pushes call transcripts and structured data to CRM automatically.

Shipped in two variants (GHL+Zapier and Retell+n8n) with swappable CRM and knowledge base. Perfect for plumbing, HVAC, and other service industries needing 24/7 call handling.
