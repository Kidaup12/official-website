Overview

Solve the cold email problem: generic messages get ignored. This system generates thousands of personalized emails using real prospect data automatically.

Scrapes data from Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and LinkedIn to generate unique emails referencing follower counts, ad spend, and competitor analysis. Runs overnight to fill your inbox with warm leads by morning.

Digital marketing agency scaled to 500+ emails/day in 2 weeks and sent 2,000+ emails in 4 days, dramatically improving reply rates and conversions.
