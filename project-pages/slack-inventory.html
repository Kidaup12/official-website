Overview

Transform manual inventory tracking into proactive, automated operations through Slack.

AI-powered system monitors stock levels, sends threshold alerts, and generates purchase orders automatically. Staff can request inventory checks and restock orders through simple Slack commands. Maintains a single source of truth for all inventory data and easily scales to WhatsApp or guest-facing channels for expanded functionality.

Key Capabilities

Stock Monitoring

Continuous AI monitoring of stock levels to prevent shortages.

Threshold Alerts

Instant notifications in Slack when items run low.

Auto-Ordering

Generates purchase orders automatically based on demand.

Slack Commands

Simple commands for staff to check status or request items.
