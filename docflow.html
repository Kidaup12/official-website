How DocFlow Works

Automated Data Extraction

Our AI engine ingests PDFs of any format—invoices, W-2s, or bank statements—and intelligently identifies key data fields. It eliminates 95% of manual data entry work instantly. The system learns your clients' specific document formats over time, becoming more accurate with every scan.

  • ✓ Processes 100+ pages per minute
  • ✓ Hand-writing recognition for annotations
  • ✓ Multi-currency and multi-language support

Direct Data Population

Numbers aren't just extracted; they are populated directly into your spreadsheets (Excel/Google Sheets) or formatted for import into accounting software like QuickBooks and Xero. No more CSV cleaning or manual re-formatting—just clean, validated data ready for review.

Integrates with: QuickBooks, Xero, Sage, NetSuite, and custom Excel templates.

Error Detection Dashboard

The system flags any low-confidence data points for human review, ensuring 99.9% accuracy. Your team only reviews the complex 3% of cases. The dashboard highlights exactly what needs attention, so you don't waste time scanning thousands of clean entries.

"Confidence Scoring" technology catches mismatches before they hit your books.

Secure Document Vault

All processed documents are encrypted and stored in an organized, searchable cloud vault. Retrieve any client document from years past in seconds. Full audit trails ensure you know exactly who accessed what and when.

Bank-grade encryption | SOC2 Type II Compliant architecture | Role-based access control

CRM & Database Connections

Connect DocFlow directly to your firm's internal databases or CRM. Ensure every client financial profile is automatically updated and synced in real-time. No more "stale data" or manual copy-pasting between systems.

Custom API and Webhook support for proprietary firm software.
