Our Story

Building systems that actually work.

In March 2025, two 25-year-old founders in Nairobi, Kenya launched KiDaFlow. No investors. No roadmap. No clients. Just a shared obsession with building systems that actually work and grow businesses.

Randy Kipkurui came from a tech background, David from engineering, and both were mind-blown by what AI could do for real businesses. They watched local companies juggle leads across WhatsApp, email, and spreadsheets—losing money every day to manual chaos.

The opportunity was obvious: bring world-class automation to growing businesses that couldn't afford to keep hiring their way out of operational bottlenecks.

Since launch, the team has built over 500 automations across AI agents, document processing systems, CRMs, and data workflows for more than 15 international businesses.

The Hard Lesson

Every great system starts with one manual process done really well—then you scale what works.

It hasn't been easy. There have been late nights, lost leads, and rebuilds. But every system taught us something new about clarity, scale, and what real impact looks like when you move beyond features and focus on outcomes.
