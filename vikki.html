Most case management software is just a digital filing cabinet. You still have to do all the manual work to move cases forward. Vikki is fundamentally different. Frequently Asked Questions

Does Vikki replace my current Case Management Software?

Vikki is designed to work alongside your existing setup (like Filevine, Litify, or Clio) or can act as your primary source of truth. It excels as a layer of automation on top of your existing digital filing cabinet.

How long does it take to implement?

Most firms are fully deployed in 2-4 weeks. This includes mapping your custom document templates and training your team on the automated workflows.

Is our client data secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and strictly adhere to data privacy standards. Your client files are stored in your own dedicated, secure Google Drive workspace.

Can I customize the documents Vikki generates?

Yes. Every document reflects your firm's specific branding, language, and legal standards. We build automation around YOUR proven templates, not generic ones.
