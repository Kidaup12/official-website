HOW VIKKI WORKS

Intake to close, one system

From the moment a case enters your firm to the day it settles, Vikki acts as an invisible paralegal. It tracks every detail, creates documents instantly, and alerts you to upcoming deadlines — making sure nothing slips through the cracks.

Why Vikki?

Most case management software is just a digital filing cabinet. You still have to do all the manual work to move cases forward. Vikki is fundamentally different. It doesn't just store documents—it reads them, processes them, writes plain-English legal responses, and automatically handles the repetitive administrative work that causes bottlenecks in PI firms.

Flawless automated documents

Stop risking human error by copy-pasting data. Vikki generates LORs, HIPAAs, engagement letters, and settlement statements instantly. Pre-filled with accurate client details, insurance data, and provider information, documents that took 20 minutes are generated in seconds.

What you get:

  • Pre-filled LORs for third-party and first-party carriers
  • Individual and bulk HIPAA generation for all providers
  • Automated settlement and payment instruction letters
  • Quick medical billing reduction requests

Relentless medical records tracking

Never rely on a sticky note to shadow a records request again. Vikki monitors every outstanding request automatically. It triggers polite follow-ups on Day 7, escalates on Day 35, and ceases immediately when records are received. Nothing is chased manually.

What you get:

  • Automated polite email follow-ups at 7 and 35 days
  • Extensive provider database with exact fax and email routing
  • Hyper-accurate billing and payment tracking
  • A firm-wide dashboard showing records status across your entire pipeline
