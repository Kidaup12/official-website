FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What do we do?

We eliminate manual tasks and help you run business operations faster and easier. Whether it involves sales outreach, complex reporting, or client communications, we use a combination of AI and automated workflows to streamine your entire operation.

Who do we help?

We focus heavily on Accounting/Finance and Legal/Law firms due to the high volume of paperwork and compliance they handle. However, we have successfully partnered with and delivered measurable results for businesses across multiple other industries as well, including Real Estate, Construction, and Home Services. Check out our Customer Success Stories to learn more.

What tools do we use?

We deploy a tailored variety of platforms to best fit your needs. This includes AI tools (like LLMs), automation engines (such as n8n and Make), alongside existing software or fully custom-built applications designed exclusively for your operations.

What are some examples of solutions we build?
  • Document reading/extractions: Automatically pull line items from PDFs and bank statements into your systems.
  • Automated CRMs: Systems that seamlessly trigger client communications across Email, SMS, and WhatsApp simultaneously.
  • Custom Operations Software: Standalone web apps and portals for managing complex client project organizations efficiently.
How can I know if you can help my business?

The best way to find out is to Schedule a Call with us. We will audit your current business processes, identify the most significant bottlenecks, and present clear opportunities for automation and growth.
