Success Story

Complete CRM Automation & Lead Management for Regional Waste Giant

Pipedrive-Centered Ecosystem for Bins4less USA

Industry: Waste Management
Location: Southern California

Project Scope

Client
Bins4less USA
Industry
Waste Management
Tech Stack
n8n, Pipedrive, CallRail, MailerLite
Core Integrations
🔹 Lead Capture (FB, Google, Yelp)
🔹 intelligent Lead Routing
🔹 Automated SMS Sequences
🔹 Historical Reactivation System

The Opportunity

Bins4less is a dumpster rental company serving Southern California with over 20 years in the business. Despite their success across Google, Bing, Facebook, and Yelp, they struggled with manual data entry and lead attribution gaps.

The Project Involvement

Kidaflow implemented a complete CRM automation and lead management system built around Pipedrive as the central hub. The system unified all lead sources—website forms, phone calls via CallRail, and ads from Facebook to Google My Business—into one automated flow.

Lead Capture & Integration

Every lead now automatically creates a deal in Pipedrive with proper source attribution. CallRail enables precise tracking, ensuring Bins4less knows exactly which marketing channel drives each dollar of ROI.

Automation & Workflow

We built intelligent lead routing that assigns deals to Customer Service Representatives (CSRs) using round-robin distribution. Automated task assignments and duplicate detection logic ensure that CSRs focus on the right leads without administrative overhead.

Reactivation & Follow-Up

We migrated historical data from CurbWaste and built reactivation sequences through MailerLite. Automated SMS sequences (2-hour, 1-day, 3-day templates) ensure high touch-point frequency for quotes without manual effort.

"Eliminating manual data entry has given Bins4less clear visibility into their marketing investments. They've reached a scale where no lead falls through the cracks."

Reporting & Analytics

  • Custom Dashboards: Real-time visibility into lead volume, conversion rates, and pipeline velocity.
  • ROI Attribution: Tracking lead value back to the specific ad platform or organic source.
  • Team Performance: Monitoring deals and follow-up efficiency by CSR.
