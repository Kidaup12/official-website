Client Overview

Cherokee CPA Services is a full-service accounting firm providing tax preparation, bookkeeping, and financial advisory services to individuals and small businesses. During peak tax season, they handle hundreds of client engagements while maintaining the personalized service their clients expect from a trusted CPA firm.

The Challenge: Tax Season Data Entry Nightmare

Like many accounting firms, Cherokee CPA faced a critical bottleneck during their busiest periods:

  • Manual data entry from bank statements, receipts, and invoices consumed 8+ minutes per document
  • CPAs were acting as data entry clerks instead of financial advisors
  • Tax season required hiring temporary staff at premium rates
  • Human error rates of 5-10% required expensive review cycles
  • Client capacity was artificially limited by processing bottlenecks

The financial impact was significant: At billable rates of $75-$100/hour, the time spent on data entry was costing the firm $15,000-$20,000 per month in lost productive capacity during peak season.

More critically: clients were arriving with "shoeboxes" of documents that needed to become organized spreadsheets. This "shoebox to spreadsheet" problem was the single biggest constraint on the firm's growth.

The Solution: DocFlow Automated Document Processing

KiDaFlow deployed DocFlow, a specialized document processing system built for finance professionals. The system uses advanced AI and OCR to transform financial documents into structured, usable data.

What the System Does

Intelligent Document Recognition

  • Automatically identifies document types (bank statements, invoices, receipts, tax forms)
  • Recognizes 50+ bank statement formats automatically
  • Handles multiple tax form types with high accuracy

Automated Data Extraction

  • Extracts dates, vendors, amounts, and categories automatically
  • Populates structured Excel sheets or pushes directly to accounting software
  • Processes documents 10x faster than manual entry
  • Batch processing for high-volume document uploads

Quality Assurance

  • Built-in validation checks flag anomalies automatically
  • Confidence scoring for extracted data
  • Simple review workflow for staff verification
  • Audit trail for all processed documents

Integration Architecture

Client Upload Portal → DocFlow OCR → AI Extraction → Validation → Accounting Software
