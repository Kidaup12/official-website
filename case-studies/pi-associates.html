Client Overview

PI Associates is a busy personal injury law firm specializing in automobile accidents, slip and fall cases, and workers' compensation claims. With three partners and a lean support team, they handle high volumes of client inquiries daily while maintaining the personalized service their clients expect.

The Challenge: $90K+ Annual Revenue Lost to Slow Response

Like many growing law firms, PI Associates faced critical bottlenecks in their client acquisition process:

  • Manual intake process required 45+ minutes per potential client
  • Response times averaged 24 hours—by which time leads had contacted competitors
  • Receptionists were drowning in paperwork while phone lines went unanswered
  • Case details were frequently lost or incomplete due to handwritten notes
  • Weekend and after-hours inquiries went unaddressed until Monday morning

The financial impact was significant: Studies show that law firms responding within 5 minutes are 100x more likely to convert leads than those responding after an hour. With an average case value of $3,000-$5,000, each lost lead represented substantial revenue.

More critically: personal injury clients are often in distress and need immediate reassurance. Delayed responses not only cost revenue but damaged the firm's reputation for client care.

The Solution: Vikki Automated Legal Intake System

KiDaFlow deployed Vikki (Kidaflow Integrated Digital Legal-system), a comprehensive intake automation platform designed specifically for law firms. The system transforms how PI Associates captures and processes new client inquiries.

What the System Does

Instant Client Engagement

Intelligent Case Processing

CRM Integration
